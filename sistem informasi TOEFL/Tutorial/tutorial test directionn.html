Test Direction

This test has two section and will take approximatelly 15 minutes to finish.

In the Reading section, you will read a passage and answer 5 questions.

In listening section, you will listen to two talks, and the answer 3 questions.

When you finish all two sections, you will see your score.

When you are ready to begin task, click on the Next button at the top righ.
