Reading Section

Direction

This section evaluates your ability to understand academic passages.

Most questions are worth one point. The instructions will indicate how many points you may receive.

You will have one question at once, you can go to the next questions byclicking Next. You can skip questions and go back to do them later. If you want to return to previous questions, click on Back.

You can click on Review at any time and you will see a review screen showing which questions you have answered and which you have not answered. From this review screen, you may go directly to any question you have already seen.

You will have 20 minutes to complete all the questions in the Reading Section.

You may now begin the Reading Section.

Click on the Next button on the top right to go on.
