Test Direction

This test has two section and will take approximatelly 40 minutes to finish.

In the Reading section, you will read a passage and answer 14 questions.

In listening section, you will listen to two talks, and the answer 11 questions.

When you finish all two sections, you will see your score.

When you are ready to begin task, click on the Next button at the top righ.
